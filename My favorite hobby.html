About My Hobby

I love photography because it allows me to capture moments, emotions, and nature in a way words can’t. I prefer taking photos of places and people rather than myself, and photography helps me feel calm and creative.

What I Photograph

Small moments that feel special and meaningful.

My Photography Tips

  • Use natural lighting whenever possible.
  • Experiment with angles and framing.
  • Look for symmetry or strong center points.
  • I use the app "Old Roll" on my phone.
